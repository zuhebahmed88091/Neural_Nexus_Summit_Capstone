Hello there! Future Shapers,

Join the ultimate AI summit for cutting-edge insights and innovation

The Neural Nexus, an AI summit, brings together a diverse community of experts, academics, and activists from the Creative Commons affiliate network. Held in a different country every two years, this event aims to foster collaboration and innovation. This year, we are excited to expand our invitation list to include organizations and individuals who share our passion for advancing the Commons, free culture, and open knowledge through collaborative projects. If you are actively involved and committed to the realms of open content and knowledge, including free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge enthusiasts, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, or activists, we warmly invite you to join us at the Neural Nexus summit. Let's come together to strengthen and invigorate the Commons, forging a brighter future collectively.

The Neural Nexus is a premier AI summit where global experts, academics, and activists from Creative Commons converge. join us to shape the future of AI through collaboration and innovation.
